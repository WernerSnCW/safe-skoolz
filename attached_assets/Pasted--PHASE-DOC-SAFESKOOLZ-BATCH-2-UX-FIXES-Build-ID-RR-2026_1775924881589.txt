**Agreed idea (one paragraph):**
Three low-effort frontend UX fixes identified in the 070 product review.
Fix 1: merge the Education and Training pages into a single "Learn" section
with two sub-tabs ("About Safeguarding" and "Using safeskoolz"), updating
