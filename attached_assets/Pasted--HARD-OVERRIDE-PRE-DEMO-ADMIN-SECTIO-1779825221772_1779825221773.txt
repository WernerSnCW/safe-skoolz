ADM-03 — Frameworks & Regulations tab
==============================================================

GOAL

  against. Pull distinct framework values from annex_templates.
  Plus the four static explainer cards below.

CONTENT TO RENDER (verbatim, framework names NOT translated)

  Card 1 — LOPIVI
    Body:
      "Spain's 2021 organic law on the integral protection of
      children and adolescents against violence. It requires
      every educational centre to appoint a designated
      wellbeing and protection coordinator and to maintain
      documented safeguarding protocols, prevention measures,
      and a clear reporting pathway for any form of violence
      against a minor."

  Card 2 — Convivèxit
    Body:
      "The Balearic Islands government's framework for school
      coexistence and conflict resolution, administered by the
      Conselleria d'Educació. It defines mandatory protocols
      for incidents including bullying, cyberbullying, and
      gender-based violence, and prescribes the documentation
      and external referral pathways that schools must follow."

  Card 3 — Keeping Children Safe in Education
    Body:
      "The UK Department for Education's statutory safeguarding
      guidance, updated annually, that all schools and colleges
      in England must follow. It requires every institution to
      designate a Safeguarding Lead, maintain a single central
      record of staff vetting, and follow a structured response
      protocol covering identify, assess, decide, and refer."

  Card 4 — Machista-violence framework (Spain)
    Body:
      "Spain's integrated framework for the prevention of and
      response to gender-based violence, anchored in Organic
      Law 1/2004 and reinforced by the State Pact against
      Gender Violence. In educational settings it requires
      schools to identify and report indicators of violence
      against women and girls, deliver age-appropriate
      prevention content, and coordinate with specialist
      external services."
