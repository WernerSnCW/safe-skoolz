## STEP 1 — ESTABLISH GOALS

No PRODUCT_GOALS.md exists. Use the operator-supplied goals below.

### Operator-supplied goals

**What this tool is for:** Safeskoolz is a school safeguarding and wellbeing
platform for Catalan/Spanish schools. It supports incident reporting, protocol
management (LOPIVI, Convivèxit, Machista Violence), pupil wellbeing monitoring,
and multi-role communication across pupils, staff, parents, SENCO, coordinators,
and PTA.

**Who uses it:** School staff (coordinators, teachers, SENCO), pupils, parents,
and PTA members. Users are not technical — the platform must be role-appropriate
and intuitive for each audience.

**What success looks like:** Every safeguarding workflow is complete end-to-end.
Each role can fulfil their responsibilities without needing to leave the platform.
The system is reliable, data-consistent, and produces no silent failures.

**What it is not for:** General school administration, lesson planning, or
anything outside the safeguarding and wellbeing remit.
